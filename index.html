IV
The Full Platform Suite

Four Tools.
One Closing OS.

Every commercial real estate closing runs on the same predictable sequence of tasks. We have engineered a dedicated instrument for each one, and wired them together so nothing falls through the gaps.

A
01 · Transaction Services

AppGen

Loan Application Generation

Commercial loan applications are structurally predictable, yet most lenders still build them by hand, in Word, with errors that trigger costly correction rounds costing two to four hours per document cycle. AppGen replaces that process entirely. A dynamic borrower intake portal adapts to property type in real time, eliminates free-text wherever possible, and auto-populates the lender's standard template the moment the borrower submits. Every negotiated term flows automatically into every corresponding loan document provision.

The first product in the market where the application and the document set are, effectively, the same document.

D
02 · Transaction Services

DocGen

Intelligent Document Drafting

DocGen holds the lender's approved document library in a secure, version-controlled vault. The moment an application executes, the platform extracts every deal term and populates every mapped field across every document. Complete coverage, no manual transcription, no missed provisions. Where judgment is required beyond field population, DocGen flags those provisions for supervising attorney review rather than guessing. The result is a first draft that arrives in minutes, not days.

A complete, accurate first draft, ready for attorney review before the kickoff call ends.

N
03 · Intelligence

NegotiateIQ

Institutional Memory Made Searchable

Every lender has made thousands of negotiation decisions across years of closings. Almost none of them are searchable. NegotiateIQ ingests your historical closed loan files, identifies every provision deviation from your standard form, and tags each one by loan size, asset class, sponsor profile, jurisdiction, and closing date. When opposing counsel submits a markup, NegotiateIQ instantly surfaces every prior instance of that provision across your entire closing history. The moat deepens with every closing.

The platform that compounds in value with every closing, and becomes more costly to leave with every year you stay.

C
04 · Transaction Services

CloseTrack

The Live Closing Command Center

The commercial real estate closing process runs on organized chaos: an Excel checklist emailed before the weekly call, document status updated manually by a paralegal working from an inbox, zero real-time visibility for any party at any moment. CloseTrack replaces all of it. A live command center, accessible to every authorized party simultaneously, generates a jurisdiction-appropriate closing checklist at deal launch. Documents are automatically checked off the correct line item the instant they arrive.

From organized chaos to orchestrated precision. One workspace where every party sees exactly what has happened and what comes next.

Built for institutions.
Ready in weeks.

The full platform stack deploys in 30 to 45 days. We are accepting a limited cohort of design-partner lenders for the inaugural launch.
